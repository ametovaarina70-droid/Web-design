About the Surgeon

Dr. Foster is a board-certified gastrointestinal and oncologic surgeon with more than 12 years of experience. She specializes in gastrointestinal tumors, colon surgery, and complex abdominal oncologic procedures.

Her approach focuses on precision, minimally invasive oncology, and compassionate communication with every patient. She participates in international research groups dedicated to GI tumor treatments and modern oncologic technologies.
