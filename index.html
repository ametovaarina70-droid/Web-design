About Nova Surgical Center

At Nova Surgical Center we combine evidence-based medicine, advanced technology, and a strong focus on safety to deliver high-quality surgical care.

Patient-centered care

Every patient receives an individual treatment plan, clear explanations, and continuous support before and after surgery.

Modern surgical techniques

We specialize in minimally invasive and laparoscopic procedures that help reduce pain and shorten recovery time.

Experienced team

Our board-certified surgeons work closely with anesthesiologists and nurses to ensure safe and efficient operations.
